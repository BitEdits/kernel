SYNOPSIS

Synrc RT is an ANSI C99 POSIX RTOS for Synrc SKYNET application, AtomVM and Sokhatsky Commander. It supports a limited curated set of hardware platforms (including simulation and emulation) and plain make buildsystem. The size of zipped source distribution is limited to 2.88MB (~200K LOC).

PLATFORMS

  • SIM
  • QEMU
  • CXD5602
  • RP2040
  • RP2350
  • RK3399
